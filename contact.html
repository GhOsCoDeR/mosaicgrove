Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place a wholesale order?

For wholesale inquiries, please email us at wholesale@mosaicgrove.com with details about your business and your order requirements. Our wholesale team will get back to you within 2 business days with pricing and minimum order information. We offer special pricing for bulk orders and can customize packaging for retail partners.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary depending on the destination and order weight. During checkout, you'll see the shipping options and costs available for your location. Please note that any import duties or taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Are your products certified organic?

Yes, all our products are certified organic by both Ghanaian and international certification bodies. We maintain strict organic farming practices, avoiding chemical pesticides and fertilizers. Our organic certification ensures that our products meet the highest standards for organic food production and processing.

How fresh are your products?

We process our nuts and superfoods in small batches to ensure optimal freshness. Each package is marked with a harvest date and best-by date. We recommend consuming our products within 6 months of purchase for the best flavor and nutritional benefits. To maintain freshness, store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Can I visit your farms in Ghana?

We offer guided tours of our farms and processing facilities for visitors interested in learning more about sustainable agriculture and our operations. Tours are available by appointment only and can be arranged by emailing tours@mosaicgrove.com at least two weeks in advance. We welcome visitors who want to see firsthand how our commitment to quality and sustainability transforms communities.
