Our Story

From humble beginnings to empowering communities in Ghana through sustainable agriculture

The Journey of Mosaic Grove

2014

A Vision Takes Root

Dr. Kaadze Wright, who left Ghana at 17 years old, returns to her homeland with a mission to address rural poverty and create sustainable economic opportunities. During her visit, she identifies cashews and tiger nuts as crops with significant potential for both local consumption and export.

2016

Research & Development

After extensive research into sustainable farming practices and market viability, Dr. Wright partners with agricultural experts to study optimal growing conditions for organic cashews and tiger nuts in Ghana's climate. The team begins small pilot projects with local farmers to test cultivation methods.

2018

Breaking Ground

Spurred by projections that Africa's working age population would increase to 1.1 billion by 2035, Mosaic Grove breaks ground on a 100-acre organic cashew farm in Ghana. This marks the beginning of a commitment to create sustainable jobs while producing high-quality, organic products.

2020

Women's Cooperative

Recognizing that women are often responsible for farming in rural Ghana, Mosaic Grove establishes the first women's cooperative farm focused on tiger nut cultivation in the eastern region of Kwahu. This initiative provides training, fair wages, and leadership opportunities for women farmers.

2021

First Harvest

The first harvest of tiger nuts marks a significant milestone, with products including tiger nut flour and milk being developed. The cooperative now employs over 25 women who receive fair wages and benefit from profit-sharing initiatives.

2022

Cashew Expansion

Twenty acres of the cashew farm are fully cultivated, with the first cashew harvest taking place. The company launches its premium organic cashew products and begins expanding distribution channels while maintaining its commitment to sustainable practices.

Today

Growing Impact

Mosaic Grove continues to grow, now employing over 50 women across both the cashew farm and tiger nut cooperative. The company is exploring new product lines and expanding its reach while staying true to its mission of empowering communities through sustainable agriculture.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Kaadze Wright

Founder & CEO

Born in Ghana and educated in the United States, Dr. Wright holds a Ph.D. in Agricultural Economics and has dedicated her career to developing sustainable farming practices that uplift communities. Her vision and leadership drive Mosaic Grove's mission forward.

Michael Chen

Founder

With extensive experience in supply chain management and sustainable business practices, Michael oversees Mosaic Grove's operations, ensuring that our commitment to quality and sustainability is maintained at every step of the process.

Abena Mensah

Farm Manager

Leading our agricultural operations in Ghana, Abena brings decades of farming expertise and deep knowledge of local growing conditions. She coordinates with local farmers and ensures our organic practices meet the highest standards.

Our Vision

At Mosaic Grove, we envision a world where sustainable agriculture becomes a pathway to economic empowerment for communities across Africa. We believe that by harnessing the power of indigenous crops like cashews and tiger nuts, we can create positive ripple effects that extend far beyond farming.

We're not just growing crops, we're growing opportunities, communities, and a sustainable future for generations to come.

Our approach combines traditional knowledge with modern agricultural science to produce superior-quality organic products while nurturing the land and providing fair wages and training for our farmers. As we look to the future, we are committed to expanding our impact, developing new product lines, and continuing to tell the story of the hard-working individuals who make Mosaic Grove possible.
